Less Salt Taco Seasoning

Does it taste as good as normal taco seasoning? No. But reducing your salt intake might mean you can enjoy a few more burritos across your lifetime, so I think it's okay.

Ingredients

Makes about 2-2.5

  • 3tbs chili powder
  • 2tbs cumin
  • 1tbs paprika
  • 2tsp oregano
  • 1tsp onion powder
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 0.5tsp black pepper

Ingredients Normalized to tsp Parts

  • 9tsp chili powder
  • 6tsp cumin
  • 3tsp paprika
  • 2tsp oregano
  • 1tsp onion powder
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 0.5tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a small container, store in a dry place
  2. Use 4tbs for 1lb of meat alongside 3/4c water, adjust to your preferences and cook times
  3. Add after meat has been browned, then simmer until reduced
