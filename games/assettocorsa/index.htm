Assetto Corsa Resources

Resources for Assetto Corsa are scattered across the internet, coming and going with time. Forums, Youtube channels, and Facebook groups lie hidden behind a clever search or a fortuitous link. Below is a never-completable list of quality mod creators, aggregators, and tools that I have compiled as of 2022Q1.

The Game

Assetto Corsa was created by Kunos Simulazioni and published by 505 Games in 2014. There are 11 DLC items, the last of which was published in 2017. All DLCs are included at a discount in the Ultimate Bundle from Steam. Assetto Corsa Competizione (2019) serves as the modern if indirect sequel, though there are always rumors of Assetto Corsa 2.

Tools

External tools, services, and products that enhance the Assetto Corsa experience

  • Content Manager: greatly improves all parts of AC, universally considered necessary
  • Custom Shaders Patch: dynamic lighting, physics improvements, scriptable weather, particles, and the ability to configure more of the game
  • Sol: enables weather, wet track, puddles, day-night cycle, etc
  • Car Tuner: change car properties like engine type, revs, aerodynamics, etc
  • Modern FOV Calculator: calculate the proper FOV given screen size and distance
  • RSR Live Timing: lap records by track
  • Track Attack: software and AC app to provide industry-comparable driving analysis
  • Crew Chief: software that acts as a Crew Chief, providing observations, corrections, and encouragement to the driver
